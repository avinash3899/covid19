India began administration of COVID-19 vaccines on 16 January 2021. As of 2 July 2021, India has administered over 344 million doses overall, including first and second doses of the currently-approved vaccines. India initially approved the Oxford–AstraZeneca vaccine (manufactured under license by Serum Institute of India under the trade name Covishield) and Covaxin (a vaccine developed locally by Bharat Biotech). They have since been joined by the Sputnik V (manufactured under license by Dr. Reddy's Laboratories) and Moderna vaccines, and other vaccine candidates undergoing local clinical trials.
